Welcome to [Your Game Name] (the “App”). Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By installing or using the App you agree to the collection and use of information as described below.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App to provide, improve, and secure the experience.

  • Level completion events: when you finish a level we log a completion event to our backend to track progress and analytics.
  • Device identifiers: a unique GUID generated for each installation to associate events with a single user instance.
  • Crash and performance data to help diagnose and fix issues.

Third‑party Services

We use third‑party services to deliver analytics and ads. These services may collect information about your device and usage.

  • Firebase — used to store level completion events and related analytics data.
  • Google services — including AdMob for advertising and other Google Play services used by the App.
  • Ad networks via AdMob mediation — AdMob may mediate ads from other ad networks; those networks may collect device and ad interaction data.

How We Use Information

  • Game functionality: to record level completions and restore progress on the same device.
  • Analytics and improvement: to analyze gameplay patterns, fix bugs, and improve features.
  • Advertising: to serve relevant ads through AdMob and mediated ad networks.
  • Security and abuse prevention: to detect and prevent fraud or misuse.

Firebase and Unique GUID

When you first open the App we generate a unique GUID for that installation. That GUID is used only to associate level completion events and related analytics with the same installation. We send level completion events and the GUID to our Firebase project. The GUID is not linked to your name, email, or other direct personal identifiers unless you explicitly provide them through an in‑app feature.

If you uninstall the App and reinstall it, a new GUID may be generated unless you restore from a backup that preserves app data.

Advertising and AdMob

We display ads in the App using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID), and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads.

AdMob mediation and other ad services

AdMob can mediate ads from multiple ad networks. When mediation is enabled, ads shown in the App may be served by third‑party ad networks that AdMob supports. Those networks may include, but are not limited to, major partners commonly used with AdMob mediation (for example: AdColony, AppLovin, Unity Ads, IronSource, Chartboost and others). Each mediated network has its own data collection and privacy practices.

Important: The exact set of mediated networks can change over time and may vary by region and configuration. For details about AdMob, mediation, and the list of supported networks, please consult Google’s AdMob documentation and the privacy policies of the individual ad networks.

Data Retention and Security

  • Retention: We retain level completion events and related analytics for as long as needed to provide the App and improve it. We periodically purge or aggregate older data.
  • Security: We use industry standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Choices
